Ukrainian Civil Society Hub

We are a Ukrainian volunteer hub in Brussels. The time of war caught us abroad, but all of us are here - one team. Our hearts belong to our people.

Our mission is to help Ukraine and Ukrainians in every possible way. Each of us is doing our best to help each other in these difficult times.

We will be glad to all those who are interested in cooperation and want to help Ukraine.

Our address: Station Europe, European Parliament visitors center, Pl. du Luxembourg, 1050 Ixelles, Belgium.

Our e-mail: ua.civilsocietyhub@gmail.com

Together we will win!!!
